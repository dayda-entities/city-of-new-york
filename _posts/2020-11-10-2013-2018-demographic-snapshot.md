Enrollment counts are based on the October 31st Audited Register for each school year.
   * Please note that October 31st enrollment is not audited for charter schools or Pre-K Early Education Centers (NYCEECs). Charter schools are required to submit enrollment as of BEDS Day, the first Wednesday in October, to the New York State Department of Education.
